Annual Dues Notice

The 2022 annual assessment of $50.00 was due on 03/01/2022. Payments can be made via check to the PO Box.

Make your check to:
Carriage Hills Community Association

Mail to:
Carriage Hills Homeowners
2977 Hwy.K #128
O'Fallon 63368

Depending on your financial institution, you may also be able to set up the HOA through your online bill pay system and request a paper check be mailed for payment on your behalf. You will need to research this option with your specific bank.

You can also electronically transfer your payment to the HOA account. Please email carriagehillssubdivision@gmail.com for details on the electronic transfer.

We appreciate the majority of homeowners pay their dues on time!

You should know: Unpaid dues accrue interest and fees are added if it is necessary to pursue collection and for a lien to be recorded. Please keep costs down by paying your dues promptly!
