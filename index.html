Architecture Control Committee

Looking to put in a fence, shed, deck, patio or other structure? You will need to submit your project for approval by the architecture control committee. Here are the steps before starting the project:

  • Review city guidelines.
  • Review convenants.
  • Obtain any necessary permits.
  • Complete Architecture Review Submission form.
  • Email form to carriagehillsacc@gmail.com.
  • ACC will contact you with next steps.
