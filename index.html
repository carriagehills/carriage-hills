Reminders

The bylaws do not allow for boats, campers, trailers, or other recreational vehicles to be kept or stored on the lot (unless stored in an enclosed garage). If you are doing temporary maintenance or packing/unpacking from a trip, please proactively email the ACC with your plans and date of completion. This saves the frustration of a homeowner unexpectedly receiving a violation letter in the mail. Thank you!

If you plan to install a shed, fence, patio, or other structure, follow the Architectural Control Committee Process.
