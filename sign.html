` Carriage Hills Subdivision - Sign

Sign Proposal Information

Background

The wooden inserts on the monument signs are original from the late 1980's and are 35+ years old. These inserts are deteriorating and have rotted beyond restoration or repair. This vote is to determine a direction for the current monument signs located at White Chapel & Weldon Spring Road.

Investigating Options to Move Forward

  • Replace current wooden sign inserts with a durable weather-resistant and modern High Density Urethane material.(estimated between 5k-9k. Information is still being gathered. Final price and information will be sent out once finalized.)
  • Tear down existing signs/brick structures/excavate concrete pads/remove landscaping. Plot would be seeded with grass. No replacement monuments or signage would be considered at this time.(estimated around $3,500.)
  • Leave the signs as-is. No repair work or replacement would be performed.

    • The goal is to utilize the current funds homeowners have been paying in over the years for this improvement project. More information will be shared once it is finalized.
