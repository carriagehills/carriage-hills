Sign Proposal Information

Background

The wooden inserts on the monument signs are original from the 1980's and are 35+ years old. These inserts have been deteriorating and are beyond repair. This vote is for the homeowners to determine a direction for the current monument signs located at White Chapel & Weldon Spring Road.

Cash on-hand from the subdivision account homeowners have been paying into over the years is expected to be used for this project. No extra annual cost would be incurred by homeowners for the project.

Proposed Options

Replace (Cost Pending: Est. 5k-9k)

Current wooden sign inserts will be upgraded with a durable weather-resistant High Density Urethane sign and modernized design. This option is only to upgrade the wooden inserts. The brick monument structures would remain unchanged

Demolish ($3,500)

All structures would be removed. There would be no signage designating the subdivision as Carriage Hills in place. The existing signs, brick structures, concrete pads, and landscaping would be excavated. Plot would be seeded with grass. No replacement monuments or signage would be considered at this time.

Leave As-Is

No repair work or replacement would be performed.
